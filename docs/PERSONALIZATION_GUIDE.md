# JAG-R018 Personalization Guide

## Tone

Personalization should feel helpful and lightweight:

- 适合带老人慢慢体验
- 适合亲子家庭
- 适合情侣散步
- 适合美食探索
- 适合文化体验

Avoid making the UI feel like a complex tag system.

## Pacing Rules

### Elder

- fewer stops
- slower rhythm
- rest notes
- lower walking pressure

### Family

- lighter route
- interactive or relaxed stops
- avoid dense schedules

### Couple

- scenic rhythm
- walking and photo space
- softer evening-friendly narrative

### Foodie

- more food insertion
- route should not be only spots

### Culture

- more background story
- connect places to traditions and history

## Recommendation Ethics

- Do not force sponsor results.
- Do not overfit based on one word.
- Do not imply sensitive personal attributes.
- Do not hide practical constraints like walking intensity.

## Current Boundary

R018 does not connect real AI, maps, PostgreSQL, Redis, payment, real booking, DaDa dispatch, ads, or user accounts.

# JAG-R017 Travel Narrative Guide

## Voice

Japan AI Guide should sound like a calm AI guide:

- direct
- practical
- route-aware
- not overly promotional
- not technical

## Itinerary Narrative

Good itinerary text explains why the order works.

Examples:

- 上午先去清水寺，适合作为京都路线的第一个重点。
- 中段安排美食或休息，避免整天只做景点打卡。
- 下午继续到伏见稻荷，让文化背景和现场体验连起来。

## Pacing

Route storytelling should mention:

- morning and afternoon rhythm
- rest and food insertion points
- avoiding repeated backtracking
- not overloading a half-day plan

## Recommendation Tone

Use user-facing reasons:

- 适合第一次来京都的人。
- 适合直接作为半日或一日路线参考。
- 适合在参观前理解文化背景。

Avoid internal language:

- ranking_score
- matched_by
- cache hit
- orchestration strategy
- token gate

## Current Boundary

R017 uses heuristic and template route composition only. It does not connect real AI, maps, PostgreSQL, Redis, payment, hotel booking, DaDa dispatch, or real partner ranking.
